3D Equation Grapher (1998)
Software to render 3D equations with distributed computing that I did with my friend
as part of a project back in university..
It had the following features:
  • Equation parsing with trigonometric function and exp. fction support
  • Dot, Wireframe, Colored and Light Vector Drawing Modes
  • Zoom, move, Rotate
  • Distributed Computing support by the use of clients installed over a network
    where the main application ditributes the computation load over the network.

To Enter an equation Click on Graph->Settings the Enter an Equation and Click Update.
Equation Operators: * / - + l e c s t
Examples: 2cx*sy , x+l(y-(sx)+2) , cx*sy , x*2sy
